PHOTOSHOP

Movie poster

This project was one of the first Photoshop projects I ever did. The goal of the assignment was to take a picture of the professor in a suit, with a greenscreen behind, and turn it into a movie poster. The name "The Kid" I feel identified with, because one of the current players in the team I support has this as a nickname. And he isn't just a player, his name is Fernando Torres, legend of the club.

Where the car go?

This project was the first Photoshop project that I was ever really proud of. The assingment was to change the story of an image. I thought this would be a nice change, reminding us how a nice view can be found, in nature, even if technology tries to take that away from us sometimes.

ANIMATIONS

This is the sum of most of my animation projects. Using after effects, tracking and keying, the 1.2 keylight effect for greenscreen, the pin tool, some effects and some photoshop skills to create the main characters of the animations.
